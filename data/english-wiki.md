# English Learning Wiki

This wiki tracks recent English-study posts so the generator can manage difficulty, avoid repetition, and keep the blog format consistent.

## Rules

- Avoid trivial sentences such as `I am happy.`
- Progress gradually across roughly `500 -> 900` TOEIC-style difficulty.
- Avoid reusing the same core expression within 14 days when possible.
- Avoid reusing the same grammar point within 7 days when possible.
- Avoid reusing the same exact sentence within 30 days when possible.
- Every sentence should contain at least one meaningful learning point.

## Difficulty Ladder

- `500-600`: basic tense control, modal verbs, common prepositions
- `600-700`: gerunds/infinitives, relative clauses, common phrasal verbs
- `700-800`: conditionals, participle structures, modal perfect, nuance in discussion
- `800-900`: inversion, ellipsis, advanced connective phrasing, subtle contrast
